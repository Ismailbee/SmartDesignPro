### Auto Design Dropdown won't close?
- Click outside the dropdown
- Click the "Auto Design" button again
- Refresh the page if issue persists

### More Menu Modal won't close?
- Press the **Escape** key
- Click the **X** button in the top-right
- Click the **dark backdrop** area
- Refresh the page if issue persists

### Can't find a specific template?
- Scroll through the Auto Design dropdown
- All 23 categories are listed alphabetically
- Use the scrollbar if needed

### Navigation not working?
- Make sure you're clicking the menu item, not just hovering
- Check your internet connection
- Try refreshing the page
