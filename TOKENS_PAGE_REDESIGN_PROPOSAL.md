## Current Design Issues

### ‚ùå Problems Identified:
1. **Weak Visual Hierarchy** - All sections have similar visual weight

## Proposed Design Improvements

### 1. **Hero Section with Account Overview**
- **Large, prominent token balance display** with animated counter
- **Current plan badge** with clear status
- **Quick stats** (designs created, tokens used)
- **Gradient background** for visual impact
- **Responsive design** that works on mobile

### 2. **Improved Visual Hierarchy**
- **Primary CTA** (Buy Tokens) - Bold, prominent
- **Secondary CTAs** (Upgrade Plan) - Subtle but accessible
- **Tertiary actions** - Minimal styling
- **Clear section separation** with proper spacing

### 3. **Modern Card Design**
- **Subtle shadows** for depth (not heavy)
- **Hover animations** for interactivity feedback
- **Gradient accents** on premium options
- **Icon-based visual indicators**
- **Smooth transitions** for professional feel

### 4. **Enhanced Token Packages**
- **Larger, clearer pricing** (‚Ç¶ amount)
- **Token count prominently displayed**
- **Value indicator** (tokens per ‚Ç¶)
- **"Best Value" badge** with visual emphasis
- **Hover effects** showing savings

### 5. **Subscription Plans Section**
- **Comparison-friendly layout**
- **Feature highlights** with checkmarks
- **Clear pricing** (monthly/yearly)
- **Recommended plan** highlighted
- **Upgrade path** clearly indicated

### 6. **Color Scheme**
- **Primary**: Modern blue (#635BFF or similar)
- **Success**: Green for active/current
- **Warning**: Amber for "Best Value"
- **Neutral**: Grays for secondary info
- **Gradients**: Subtle, professional

### 7. **Typography**
- **Headlines**: Bold, clear hierarchy
- **Body**: Readable, proper line-height
- **Numbers**: Large, prominent
- **Labels**: Small, secondary color

### 8. **Spacing & Layout**
- **Generous padding** (24px, 32px, 48px)
- **Consistent gaps** between sections
- **Responsive grid** (1 col mobile, 2-3 col desktop)
- **Max-width container** for readability

### 9. **Interactive Elements**
- **Smooth hover states** on cards
- **Loading animations** for purchases
- **Success confirmations** with animations
- **Error states** with clear messaging
- **Disabled states** clearly indicated

### 10. **Trust & Security**
- **Secure payment badge** (Paystack)
- **Money-back guarantee** text
- **SSL/Security indicators**
- **Customer testimonials** (optional)
- **FAQ section** (optional)
